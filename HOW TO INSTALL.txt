# HexHUD

HOW TO DOWNLOAD;

Download the 'zip' folder from Github, and extract it anywhere easily accessible for you.
Cut the "HexHud" folder from this extract
Paste it into "C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Team Fortress 2\tf\custom"

***If you aren't using any 'alternative' version, feel free to cut the folder out elsewhere, or just delete it! It can impact start-up times of your game!!!***

Enjoy the HUD!!
